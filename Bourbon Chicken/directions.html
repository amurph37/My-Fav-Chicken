  1. Clean your Chicken properly in Salt, water, and vinegar (or lemon juice)
  2. Season Chicken with
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Poultry Seasoning
    • 1 Tablespoon of Hoisin Sauce
  3. After seasoning and mixing in your Hoision Sauce
    • You are going to sear your chicken for 15 mins in Medium-high heat on each side.
  4. Now you will need to cut (into strips or dices) or shred (based on your preference) your chicken.
  5. Now you will need to prepare your Bourbon Sauce
  6. You will need to mix together
    • 1/3 cup of Hoisin Sauce
    • 1/3 cup of Soy Sauce
    • 2 TBSP butter
    • 1 Tablespoon of Minced Garlic
    • 1 Tablespoon of Ginger
    • 1/4 cup of Brown Sugar
  7. Now you will need to sir and simmmer the sauce for 20 mins
    • Under Medium-high heat
  8. Mix in your chicken once the sauce is completed.

Once you mix in your chicken with your bourbon sauce, you can pair this delicous dish with Rice, Noodles, Vegatables, etc. (Based on your personal preference).
